About Watseka Community Renewal

Watseka Community Renewal (WCR) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to preserving and revitalizing the historic community of Watseka, Illinois.

Our Mission

We support small businesses and community-driven projects that strengthen Watseka's economy, culture, and future. Through grants, partnerships, and hands-on initiatives, we work to ensure that Watseka remains a vibrant place to live, work, and grow.

What We Do

  • Small Business Grants — We provide funding to help local businesses invest in renovations, beautification, marketing, signage, and equipment.
  • Historic Preservation — We work to preserve and restore the historic structures that define Watseka's downtown and surrounding neighborhoods.
  • Community Development — We collaborate with local organizations, government, and residents to improve public spaces and foster community pride.

501(c)(3) Status

Watseka Community Renewal is a federally recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Our Vision

We envision a Watseka where historic buildings are preserved and repurposed, local businesses thrive, and residents take pride in their community. Every project we support is a step toward that future.

Get Involved

Whether you want to apply for a grant, make a donation, or simply reach out to learn more, we welcome your involvement. Together, we can renew Watseka.
