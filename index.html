What We Do

Watseka Community Renewal is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to preserving and revitalizing our community.

Small Business Support

We provide grants and resources to help local businesses improve their storefronts, invest in marketing, and grow their operations.

Community Revitalization

We work to preserve and restore historic structures and public spaces that define Watseka's character and heritage.

Building Our Future

Through partnerships and community-driven projects, we invest in initiatives that strengthen Watseka's economy and culture.
